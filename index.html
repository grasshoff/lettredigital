Enhancements

Fine academic books computationally enhanced

Open access

01

Digitised prints of books, articles, manuscripts or memos are complemented by computational content: annotations, memos, references, literature, 3D, animated and interactive.

Deep reading

02

Deep reading segments the flow of words, images, cross-links, footnotes into sentences and meaningful units. It parses their grammar and performs a semantical analysis.

Scholarversum

03

The universe of science and scholarship is linked with thoughts, phrases and point of interests. Bibliographical references enrich arguments. Scholarversum gathers reference material. Deep reading identifies thoughts and attaches them to the texts of the computational book.

Structured document

04

Chapters, pages, sentences, images, footnotes, equations, chemical formulas, drawings or sketches. Their sophisticated design transfers to a well-understood structure of expressions that can be processed by modern machine learning technology.

Visual

05

Cognitive processing parses textual expressions as deep patterns. Such thoughts are interlinked, connected with images and memory. Deep reading unlocks their understanding. Visuals express those thoughts and guide the mental discourse.

Languages

06

Translating languages is a form of speaking: The author to her reader - the historian to his sources - the argument to its premisses. The language module assists these active textual communication.
